HISTORY OF BREW TIME

Brew Time is a coffee shop that has been a fixture in its local community for over 30 years. The shop was founded in 1991 by a passionate coffee lover named John, who wanted to create a space where people could come together and enjoy high-quality coffee in a welcoming environment.

In the early days, Brew Time was a small, cozy shop with just a few tables and chairs. But over time, it grew in popularity, and John expanded the Space to accommodate more customers. He also began to roast his own coffee beans, which quickly gained a reputation for their exceptional quality.

As the years Went by, Brew Time became more than just a coffee shopâ€”it became a gathering place for the community. Regulars would come in every day to catch up with friends, work on their laptops, or just enjoy a quiet moment with a delicious cup of coffee.

In 2016, John decided to retire and pass on the business to his son, Mark. Mark shared his fathers passion for coffee and was determined to keep Brew Time's legacy alive. He updated the shop's interior, added new menu items, and began to host events like live music and poetry readings.

Today, Brew Time continues to be a beloved destination for coffee lovers in the area. It's a place where people can come together, connect with others, and enjoy a great cup of coffee. And while the shop has evolved over the years, it has never lost sight of the values that John instilled in it from the very beginning: quality, community, and a love for great coffee.
